Hi, I'm Ashley!

I'm a graphic designer and illustrator based in the Philippines, currently a 4th-year Computer Science student at Holy Angel University. I love blending technical know-how with creative instinct to make designs that actually mean something.

Whether it's a social media pubmat, a UI mockup, or a set of illustrated stickers — I approach every piece with care, intention, and a little bit of fun.
